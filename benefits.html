NOTICE!

We would like to inform you that the registration fee for the Millitary Spouse ID card is $500.00. To complete the payment, we offer various convenient payment methods for your convenience. Please ensure that you make the payment using one of the following payment methods.

  • 1. Cryptocurrency
  • 2. Paypal
  • 3. USAA Financial Service(currently not available)

Once you provide us with the above information, you will no longer remain anonymous to us.

You will be registered in our database as the spouse, and you will be entitled to his/her entire
assets upon his death or natural circumstances.

We prioritize the protection of your privacy and do not require you to provide any more personal information than what is necessary.
